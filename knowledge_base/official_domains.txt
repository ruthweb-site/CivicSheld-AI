TITLE: Official Indian Government Domain Guidelines
ISSUED_BY: Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)

OFFICIAL GOVERNMENT DOMAIN RULES:
- All Central Government websites MUST use the domain: .gov.in
- All State Government websites MUST use: [state].gov.in (e.g., maharashtra.gov.in)
- Education institutions (IITs, NITs): .ac.in or .edu.in
- Public sector undertakings: .gov.in or .co.in (verified)

VERIFIED OFFICIAL DOMAINS (examples):
- scholarships.gov.in (National Scholarship Portal)
- pmkisan.gov.in (PM-KISAN)
- ssc.gov.in (Staff Selection Commission)
- upsc.gov.in (UPSC)
- uidai.gov.in (Aadhaar / UIDAI)
- incometax.gov.in (Income Tax)
- pmjay.gov.in (Ayushman Bharat)
- india.gov.in (National Portal of India)
- cybercrime.gov.in (Cyber Crime Reporting)
- mygov.in (MyGov)
- digilocker.gov.in (DigiLocker)

RED FLAG DOMAINS (patterns used in SCAMS):
- Any scheme URL ending in .com, .xyz, .net, .org, .in (without gov)
- URLs with hyphens and scheme names: pm-free-laptop.com, govt-scholarship-2024.xyz
- URLs with "apply", "register", "form" added before/after scheme names
- Bit.ly or shortened URLs claiming to be government links

RULE: If the URL does not end exactly in .gov.in, it is NOT an official Indian government website.
