DOCUMENT_ID: GOV-SCH-001
SOURCE: National Scholarship Portal (NSP) | scholarships.gov.in
TITLE: Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students
ISSUED_BY: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment
VALID_UNTIL: 31 March 2025
OFFICIAL_DOMAIN: scholarships.gov.in

ELIGIBILITY:
- Applicant must belong to Scheduled Caste (SC) community.
- Annual family income must not exceed Rs. 2,50,000.
- Applicant must have passed Class 10 (Matric) examination.
- Applicant must be studying in a recognized institution.

APPLICATION PROCESS:
- Apply ONLY through the official portal: https://scholarships.gov.in
- No physical application is accepted.
- Documents required: Income certificate, Caste certificate, Aadhaar card, Bank passbook (with IFSC code).
- Last date for application: 31 October each year.

PAYMENT:
- Scholarship amount is credited directly to the student's bank account via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).
- No agent or middleman is involved. Government never asks for money to process applications.

WARNING:
- The government does NOT send scholarship offers via WhatsApp or SMS.
- Any website other than scholarships.gov.in is NOT official.
- Do NOT share OTP, ATM PIN, or bank details with anyone claiming to process your scholarship.
