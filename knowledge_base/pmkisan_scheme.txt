TITLE: PM-KISAN Scheme Official Guidelines
ISSUED_BY: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers' Welfare
OFFICIAL_DOMAIN: pmkisan.gov.in

SCHEME OVERVIEW:
- PM-KISAN provides income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to eligible farmer families.
- Amount is paid in 3 equal instalments of Rs. 2,000 each directly to the bank account.
- No application fee. Completely free scheme.

ELIGIBILITY:
- All landholding farmer families with cultivable land.
- Excludes: Government employees, income tax payers, professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers).

HOW TO APPLY:
- Register ONLY at: https://pmkisan.gov.in
- Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) for help. Free of cost.
- Required documents: Aadhaar, Bank account details, Land records.

PAYMENT VERIFICATION:
- Check your payment status at: https://pmkisan.gov.in/beneficiarystatus.aspx
- All payments are done via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).
- Government never calls to ask for OTP or bank account number.

OFFICIAL HELPLINE: 155261 / 1800115526 (Toll Free)

SCAM WARNING:
- No WhatsApp group or Facebook page is operated by PM-KISAN officially.
- Websites ending in .com, .xyz, .net claiming to be PM-KISAN are FAKE.
- The official domain is pmkisan.gov.in ONLY.
- Do NOT pay any registration fee. The scheme is 100% free.
