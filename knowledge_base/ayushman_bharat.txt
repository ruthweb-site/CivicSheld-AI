TITLE: Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) Guidelines
ISSUED_BY: National Health Authority
OFFICIAL_DOMAIN: pmjay.gov.in | beneficiary.nha.gov.in

SCHEME OVERVIEW:
- PM-JAY provides health cover of Rs. 5 lakh per family per year.
- Covers secondary and tertiary hospitalization at empanelled hospitals.
- 100% cashless treatment. No premium to be paid by the beneficiary.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Based on SECC (Socio-Economic Caste Census) 2011 data.
- Includes rural deprived families and urban workers in specified occupations.
- Check eligibility: https://pmjay.gov.in OR call 14555.

HOW TO ACCESS:
- Visit any empanelled government or private hospital.
- Show Aadhaar card or PM-JAY e-card (Golden Card).
- Get Golden Card from: Common Service Centre (CSC) or District Hospital.

EMPANELLED HOSPITALS: https://hospitals.pmjay.gov.in

OFFICIAL HELPLINE: 14555 (Toll Free, 24x7)

SCAM WARNING:
- Government does NOT ask for money to issue the Ayushman Bharat card.
- Do NOT pay touts or agents to get the golden card. It is FREE.
- Websites other than pmjay.gov.in are NOT official.
- Government never contacts via WhatsApp to issue or renew cards.
- Do NOT share OTP or Aadhaar details with unauthorized persons.
